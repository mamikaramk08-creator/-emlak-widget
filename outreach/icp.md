# Ideal Customer Profile — US Cold Outreach Campaign

## Who we're targeting

Independent / boutique US real estate brokerages, roughly **1–50 agents**,
that have a website but **no visible live-chat or lead-capture widget**.

## Why this ICP

- Narrow, specific targeting outperforms generic targeting: ~13% reply rate
  for small businesses without a website/tool vs. ~5-8.5% generic (per the
  lead-gen playbook).
- At this size, the broker-owner is usually the buyer. They reply to their
  own email directly — no procurement layer, no marketing department to get
  past.
- No live chat installed = a visible, easy-to-explain gap this product
  fills, and the pitch requires no live spoken conversation, which fits the
  campaign's async-only CTA constraint (see `email-sequence.md`).

## Disqualifiers

- Corporate/franchise HQ accounts (RE/MAX corporate, Compass corporate,
  Coldwell Banker corporate, etc.) — target independents only.
- Sites that already have a visible live-chat widget (Intercom, Drift,
  Tidio, etc.) — deprioritize, don't hard-exclude, since a manual check
  isn't 100% reliable.

See `apollo-filters.md` for the exact lead-sourcing filter values.
